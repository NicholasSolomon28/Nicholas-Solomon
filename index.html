History

EcoPure was founded in 2017 by Nick Solomon, an environmental entrepreneur and student at the University of New Hampshire. What began as a small operation selling biodegradable cleaning products at local markets has since grown into a nationally recognized brand. From reusable kitchenware to compostable packaging, EcoPure has evolved into a full-spectrum sustainable lifestyle company.
